MED-EBOOK

“He who has health has hope; and he who has hope has everything.”






Due to Covid-19 Pandemic, every person is extremely conscious about their health and people close to them. Our project is mainly focused on people’s health. We decided to design a webpage which allows the user to register and login. We ask few personal and medical data from the user. User needs to update their medical data monthly. In case of any health issues (from the medical data), solutions are suggested. A list of nearby clinics/hospitals will also be provided. Also, in case of blood requirement, we provide contact details of users who are willing to donate which is based on the Blood Group requirement and location

About

Med-Ebook PORTAL WANTS TO BE SIMPLE is an initiative to help people to maintain good medical records. Our system will help our patients to better manage their care, such as Blood Group, Blood sugar levels, pulse rate, lab results, BMI, Weight, etc. – in electronic form as makes it easy for patients to update and share their records.
